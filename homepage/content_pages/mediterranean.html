Important ingredients of Mediterranean food

Mediterranean cuisine is famous all over the world. Moreover, some food experts claim that Mediterranean cuisine has three core elements: olive, wheat, and grape. These food items are typically made of the light and flavorful ingredients of the Mediterranean region and include fruits, vegetables, grains, white meat, fish, as well as dairy.
